Indian Festivals



Diwali

Diwali is the Hindu festival of lights. It is generally celebrated in October and November. It is celebrated because Lord Ram came to Ayodhya after defeating Ravan



Holi

Holi is the Hindu festival of colours. It marks the coming of spring.It is celebrated in March.



Eid

Eid is the Muslim Festival of breaking fasts. It is celebrated after the month of Ramadan, in which people fast from sunrise to sunset. It is commemorated by a large, community-wide prayer service in the morning, followed by meals and conversation with friends and family.
